About Me

Dedicated to improving animal welfare through research and ethical practice

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the University of British Columbia, conducting interdisciplinary research that bridges animal welfare science, ethics, and agricultural practices. My work focuses on addressing complex challenges in dairy farming, particularly around surplus dairy calf management.

With previous roles as Dairy Beef and Animal Welfare Manager at HW Greenham & Sons (2023-2025) and National Animal Welfare Lead at Dairy Australia (2019-2023), I bring practical industry experience to my research. I believe in the importance of evidence-based approaches to improving animal welfare and fostering dialogue between stakeholders across the agricultural value chain.
